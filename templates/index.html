Vinnie's Pizza Proposal


Uncle Vinnie is a profitable pizza shop owner, who is ready to expand! We were thrilled when he asked us to research information for his second location! Few restaurants last more than a few years, but for those that turn a healthy profit, expansion is a sensible next step and the location is very important! While some would say it’s best to establish the second shop in the same type of area as the first…Uncle Vinnie does not love his neighbors and thinks it would be best to branch out of state. We put together this proposal full of pizza pie data, maps and icons to help the busy, pizza man make a great decision.
