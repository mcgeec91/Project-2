Vinny's Pizza Proposal

Pizza! Every American is familiar with the word. It conjures up multiple preferences, whether a unique around-the-corner provider or any of the many nationwide chains. Pizza may have had its origins in Italy, but it was perfected by Americans (or its Italian immigrants). Anyone who has visited Italy will attest to this truth. Today, with vegan and gluten free alternatives, everyone gets to select the pizza that suits their lifestyle. In fact, every second of every day in the United States 350 slices of pizza is being consumed! The focus for this report is Uncle Vinnie. He is a profitable restaurateur with one location poised for expansion. While it is a fact that most restaurants fail within the first few months, for those that turn a healthy profit, expansion is a sensible step. However, location is a critical factor. We have been challenged to make recommendations for the second location. Research included to make a conclusion are location (latitude and longitude), pricing tables (maximum and minimum), demographics for pedestrian and automobile traffic and proximity to office and residential communities. Methodology used was extracting data from Kaggle.com for analysis. (Our team experienced some difficulty gathering data due to the recent government shutdown.) We assembled a full proposal of pizza pie data, maps and icons to provide the busy, pizza man details to make an informed decision.
